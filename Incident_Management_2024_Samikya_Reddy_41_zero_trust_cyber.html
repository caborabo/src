Revolutionizing Incident Management with Zero Trust Architecture: A Data-Driven Approach to Mitigating Cyber Threats

Abstract

Discover how Zero Trust Architecture is transforming incident management and reshaping cybersecurity! Learn how ZTA principles like “never trust, always verify” and cutting-edge technologies like AI and blockchain can slash threat response times and reduce breaches.
